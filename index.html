My Fitness Routine

Inclined bench presses

The Smith Machine is a docking station of your entire workout routine. We use successive circuitry. You will do one set on the Smith Machine and then rotate out to a satellite station.

We recommend 5 repetitions.

Counter-weighted dips

The Dip Assist Machine is your station for upper shoulders, arms, and back strength. It is counterbalanced with offsetting weights, so that the dip can be done comfortably.

We recommend 5 repetitions.

Shop Flexa Gym Grips →
Inclined leg presses

The Leg Press Machine is the only station needed for core and length strength. The amount of weights should be no greater than what you can push up & down while having full control of the motion.

We recommend 5 repetitions.

Parallel bar push-ups

The Push-Up Station is fundamental for your fitness. Push-ups are done flat-handed on the floor. You should do them only if the floor parallel bars are available to prevent any injury.

We recommend 5 repetitions.

Inclined core crunches

The Adjustable Bench is the station for core crunches. Put the adjustable bench into a 65-degree angle to allow for a fully functional stomach crunch without any detrimental affects to the lower lumbar.

We recommend 5 repetitions.

Arm and wrist curles

The Olympic Bar will be exclusively used for arm and wrist curls. It is essential that the weights added to the Olympic Bar are minimal. This will allow you to have total control of the bar and movement.
